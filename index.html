Last.fm live recommender
How it works

Introduce your Last.fm API key and your user if you have scrobblings. Remember you can always look for other users' music ;)

The system will search for your top artists in the last six months and find some recommendations based on them.

Click in your top listened artists to see the recommendations based on them. Listen to the selected artists and their songs!
